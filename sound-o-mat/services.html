Foley & Sound Design
Audio Cleaning & Restoration

Foley is the art of creating sound effects and samples for film, video games and other audio productions from digital editing & manipulation of recordings of everyday objects.

The low-resolution versions below of gun sounds were created for a video game company and were sourced using metal pans, a set of keys and a deadbolt lock. Stereo/mono 44.1/48k 16/32b versions available on request.

Audio "cleaning" involves the removal of hiss, clicks, pops plus background and other unwanted noise. Sources I've worked on include reel-to-reel, cassette and 8-track tapes, Mini-Discs, live recordings and even 78rpm shellac records and recording wire.
